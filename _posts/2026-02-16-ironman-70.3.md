## Ironman 70.3 - Salem, Oregon

Here I will document my journey to completing my first half ironman. It will take place July 19 in Salem, Oregon. 

### February 22

I selected the Phil Mosley 20-week intermediate training plan and began this past Monday on 2/16. So far it's going well, and I'm actually really enjoying the structured workokuts. I'm learning a lot and excited to make this commitment and track my progress. 

### March 8

The training plan is going very well and I feel like I'm becoming a bit stronger in each area. I see my swim times improving and each long run and bike is getting easier. 

### March 22

I feel like the last two weeks have been a bit of a set back. I started feeling a lot of pain in my right shoulder while swimming and am feeling a little burnt out from all the workouts. I find it really hard to fit everything in and it's quite a lot of mental load to keep scheduling and rescheduling everything I need to do to make time for everything. Definitely feeling overwhelmed and felt a little unsatisfied with my progress.
